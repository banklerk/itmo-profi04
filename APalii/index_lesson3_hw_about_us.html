__About US__

"We are creating new digital communication products for humans and for
humans’ business. Our mission is to providing quality, innovation, excellent
customer service and the best pricing available."

Founded in 2005, IXO Systems Oy is an independent VoIP provider communications
solutions serving a range of businesses, individuals,
communities and organizations around the world.
Our new product is IZIConf, a reliable, absolutely free (cost-effective), no
registration and easy to use audio conferencing service.
IZIConf service include mobile phone application. Based on domestic dialling
in Finland with strong plans to grow in to European and CIS
countries. IZIConf supports all of its services with 24/7/365.
IZIConf it is one of the innovative product of IXO Systems Oy company
which is pioneering all-digital services and built on proprietary
media servers using both PSTN (Public Switched Telephone Network) and
advanced VoIP-based (Voice over Internet Protocol) services to deliver
high-quality audio conferencing services at the best pricing available.
